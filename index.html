FAQ

(Frequently asked questions)

Mint?

Anyone can mint a Goofie for 0 ETH. Maximum per wallet is 1. You do that by either pressing that big splurge button you just scrolled past or using mint function in contract

What are Goofies?

Goofies are 10,000 unique goofy crypto degens with Creative Common 0 licence.

Any words about the collection?

The resolution of the collection is 2000x2000 and made with over 300 attributes. Both males and females. Some have a mask, some are rare, some are fat and some are absolute legends. And yes, there is some 1o1s.

Who are the founders?

We are a group of anonymous degens with backgrounds in digital marketing, software development and animation. We met through crypto a few years ago because we all had interest in trading jpegs and memecoins. Some of you might know who we are.

Why did you create Goofies?

We love the culture and being creative. We feel that with our experience we could create something special and do things differently.
